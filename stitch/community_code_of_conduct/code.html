The Way of the Cartographer.

This document is more than a list of rules; it is the soul of our community. A compass for those navigating the beautiful, messy edges of the world.

01. Our Core Values

We believe in the Radical Authenticity of the journey. We are not here to curate a perfect life, but to document a real one. Every stain on a map, every torn page in a journal, tells a story worth telling.

Kindness over Logic

Compassion is our primary language. We prioritize human connection over being "right."

Active Curiosity

Never stop asking why. We embrace the unknown with open arms and steady feet.

02. The Pirate Code

Rules for keeping the crew safe and the journey meaningful.

anchor

Guard the Hearth

Harassment, hate speech, and exclusionary behavior are the only storms we cannot weather. We have zero tolerance for any form of discrimination.

visibility_off

Respect the Secret Spots

When sharing "Treasures," respect the privacy of local communities. Do not expose fragile ecosystems or sacred spaces without permission.

edit_document

No Counterfeit Tales

Authenticity is our currency. Do not claim others' journeys as your own. Credit the storytellers and the cartographers who came before you.

shield

Safe Passage

If you witness a violation of the Code, report it immediately. Our elders review every report with care and anonymity.
